Portfolio

Larger Projects

Escape the Unknown

Download from Google Drive!

Escape the Unknown is a Rogue-Like dungeon crawler where the Hero, an amnesiac Halfling, advances through procedurally generated levels and loot to try to discover what happened to the realm they're in -- and discover their missing past. An intense narrative, action packed gameplay, and plenty of exciting loot to earn will help the player reach their goal.

Role: Designer, Programmer

Project Duration: 6 Months

Summary of Responsibilities:

On Escape the Unknown, I was a principal designer and Programmer of many systems, including Player Systems such as health and spells, the Save system, and the procedural generation, Enemy AI and Enemy systems. I also designed the Dwarven series of levels that the player proceeds through early in the game after exiting the tutorial.

The Spells created involved working on implementing SFX to properly display to the player, altering the Enemy Health system to create elemental weaknesses, and creating visual cues to indicate status effects applied to enemies.

Smaller Projects

Your Princess Intervention

Download from Google Drive!

Made for a smaller, in-studio game jam, Your Princess Intervention is a quirky first person action-adventure game. You are the hero, tasked with saving the princess! Except maybe not every princess wants to be saved. And certainly not by someone of ... questionable morals.

Your Princess Intervention was a test case for our studio using Unreal Engine 5. Built entirely in an early-access part of the engine, we as a team quickly taught ourselves to use the engine's resources to prove it would be a valid tool for our studio.

Role: Designer, Programmer

Project Duration: 2 Weeks

Summary of Responsibilities:

I was the lead designer and programmer on Player abilities, which were entirely movement based, as well as helped designing movement puzzles. The abilities included an air dash, a teleport, and a quick sidestep to empower player combat.

I was the lead designer and programmer that implemented player progression, enabling the player to keep upgrades they acquired in levels in progressing to new levels.

Space Quasar

Made for my Bachelor's Capstone, Space Quasar is an action-platformer where the player takes on the abilities of defeated enemies and can use them to navigate the environment. This was our first project in Unreal 4, and ended up the highest reviewed in the program.

Role: Designer, Programmer

Project Duration: 4 Months

Summary of Responsibilities:

I was the lead designer for the player movement abilities, ensuring they were responsive and contributed to the player character's navigation.

I was the lead designer and programmer for the player's Consume ability, which allowed the player to defeat an enemy with a melee attack, and consume a unique ability based on the enemy.

I designed the 'Combat' level, which gave the player multiple ways to progress through the level and enabled the user to take on many different abilities and proceed how they wanted.

I designed the Player Upgrades system and created multiple different upgrades for the player to collect throughout progression, as well as missable upgrades that change what upgrades the player get throughout the linear edxperience.
